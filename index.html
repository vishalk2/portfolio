About me

Hi there! I'm Vishal, a Pre-Final Year Under Graduate Student in the Dept. of Mechanical Engineering at BITS Pilani, Goa Campus, India [2018-2022]. My academic interests lie in the field of Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD), IC Engines & Solid Mechanics. I've worked on Discrete Element Method (DEM) Simulations of Granular Particles, Engine Emissions Calibration and underwent a training on Vehicle Dynamics. I'm currently working on CFD-DEM coupling simulations using CFD-DEM solvers such as OpenFOAM & LIGGGHTS. My next project is about modelling combined joint alignments in orthoses belonging to the Bio-Mechanics domain.

As for my skills, I have experience on working with PTC Creo, AutoCAD, Ansys Workbench & Ansys APDL and OpenSCAD. I am also a Python Programmer with a fair bit of touch on having to use NumPy, Pandas and Matplotlib for my CFD works. I've had most of my CFD computations performed using MATLAB both as a part of College Curriculum as well as for External Projects.

Beyond the technical realm, I'm a Short Story and Fiction Writer very much eager to publish my works. Owing to my interests in Writing, I'm an Avid Reader by default with all my interests converging on Detective Thrillers and Mythology. I've also worked as a Content Writer as a Freelancer. My hobbies include watching & playing Cricket, binge watching Thriller Movies & Series, reading Detective and Mythological stories, and a hell lot of Imagining! My Music interests include playing Mridangam (Carnatic Music) and Conga Drums.

I'm just as curious as you are; it's just that I haven't found my answers yet!
