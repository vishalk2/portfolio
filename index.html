About me

Hi there! I'm Vishal, a Final Year Under Graduate in the Dept. of Mechanical Engineering at BITS Pilani, Goa Campus, India [2018-2022]. My academic interests lie in the field of Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) & IC Engines. As part of my CFD interest, I've worked on Discrete Element Method (DEM) Simulations of Granular Particles, worked on an IC Engines project based on Engine Emissions Calibration. I'm currently working on developing 2D mathematical models for modelling combined joint alignments in orthoses in the field of Bio-Mechanics.

As for my skills, PTC Creo is what I'm good with for a 3D modelling software skill, AutoCAD for 2D drawings and OpenSCAD for 3D printing purposes. Owing to my interests in CFD, I work with ANSYS (Fluent & APDL) in CFD applications. Python is the programming software that I work with and NumPy, Pandas & MatplotLib as additionals. I've had most of my CFD applications & computations performed using MATLAB both as a part of College Curriculum as well as for External Projects.

Beyond the technical realm, I'm a Short Story and Fiction Writer very much eager to publish my works. Owing to my interests in Writing, I'm an Avid Reader as well with all my interests resting over Detective Thrillers and Historic Fiction. I've also tried my hand in Freelancing having worked as a Content Writer for a Digital-marketing agency. My hobbies include watching & playing Cricket, binge watching Thriller Movies & Series, reading Detective and Mythological stories, writing short stories, and a hell lot of Imagining! My Music interests include playing Mridangam (Carnatic Music) and Conga Drums.

I'm just as curious as you are; it's just that I haven't found my answers yet!
